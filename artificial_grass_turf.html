THE ADVANTAGES

Artificial turf isn't just a substitute — it's a superior surface engineered for modern demands. Here's why thousands choose synthetic grass.

01
💧
Zero Water Usage
Save up to 100,000 liters of water annually per field compared to natural grass. Critical for water-scarce regions like Pakistan. No irrigation required, ever.
02
🌞
All-Weather Performance
Play in rain, heat, or cold without surface damage. UV-stabilized fibers maintain color and performance in Pakistan's extreme summer temperatures up to 55°C.
03
⏳
Long Lifespan
Premium turf lasts 10–15 years with minimal maintenance. Natural grass requires constant reseeding, resting, and replacement — dramatically increasing total cost of ownership.
04
🔧
Low Maintenance
No mowing, fertilizing, or watering. Just occasional brushing and infill top-ups. Annual maintenance costs are up to 80% lower than natural turf fields.
05
🛡️
Player Safety
Modern turf systems with shock pads meet FIFA and World Rugby concussion and abrasion standards. Consistent, even surfaces reduce ankle and knee injury risks.
06
♻️
Eco-Responsible
No pesticides, herbicides, or chemical fertilizers required. Our lead-free, non-toxic fibers are fully recyclable at end of life, with zero chemical runoff into soil or water.

FAQs

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about artificial grass, turf installation, and maintenance.

How long does artificial turf last?
+

High-quality artificial turf typically lasts 10–15 years depending on usage intensity and maintenance. Sports fields with heavy traffic last 8–12 years, while landscape turf can last up to 15–20 years.

Is artificial grass safe for children?
+

Yes. Our playground and landscaping turf is lead-free, non-toxic, and tested to EN1177 safety standards. Shock-absorbing foam underlays reduce fall impact, making it safer than concrete or even hard natural ground.

How does artificial turf handle Pakistani summers?
+

All our products use UV-stabilized fibers that resist fading and degradation in temperatures up to 55°C. Surface temperature can be reduced with light watering, and our cooling infill technology helps manage heat retention.

What maintenance does artificial turf need?
+

Minimal maintenance is required — brushing the fibers upright every few months, removing debris, and topping up infill annually. We provide full maintenance packages and training at handover.

Can turf be installed on rooftops?
+

Absolutely. Rooftop installations are a speciality of ours. We use lightweight drainage mats and bonding systems designed for concrete or membrane surfaces, making rooftop gardens and play areas easy to achieve.

How long does installation take?
+

A standard football pitch takes 5–10 working days. A residential garden can be completed in 1–2 days. Timeline depends on ground preparation needs, surface size, and any drainage work required.

Do you offer warranty?
+

Yes — we offer a 10-year manufacturer warranty on all products covering UV degradation, color retention, and fiber integrity. Installation workmanship is warranted for 2 years from completion.

Is artificial turf environmentally friendly?
+

Our turf eliminates the need for water, pesticides, fertilizers, and mowing fuel. All products are lead-free and recyclable at end of life. A single field saves over 1 million liters of water per year vs natural grass.
