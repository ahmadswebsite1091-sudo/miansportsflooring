Common Questions

BASKETBALL FLOORING FAQs

Everything you need to know before investing in a professional basketball court in Pakistan.

What is the best basketball court flooring for indoor use in Pakistan?+
For indoor basketball courts in Pakistan, maple hardwood flooring is the gold standard — the same material used in NBA arenas. It delivers superior ball bounce, excellent shock absorption, and a prestigious look. For budget-conscious projects, synthetic PU or sports vinyl provides great performance at a fraction of the cost. We recommend hardwood for professional academies and clubs, and PU/vinyl for schools and community centers.
How much does basketball court flooring cost in Pakistan?+
Basketball court flooring costs vary by material: sports vinyl from Rs. 55/sq.ft, EPDM rubber from Rs. 65/sq.ft, modular PP tiles from Rs. 75/sq.ft, synthetic PU from Rs. 90/sq.ft, concrete/acrylic from Rs. 110/sq.ft, and maple hardwood from Rs. 180/sq.ft. A full standard indoor court (28m×15m = 420 sqm) ranges from Rs. 2.3M to Rs. 8M+ depending on surface. Contact us for a free site-specific quotation.
What are the standard dimensions of a basketball court?+
FIBA standard court dimensions are 28m (length) × 15m (width). For NBA regulations, courts are 28.65m × 15.24m. We also install half courts (14m×15m), FIBA 3x3 courts (15m×11m), and fully custom sizes. All dimensions come with professional court marking included as standard.
How long does basketball court installation take?+
Modular PP tiles install in 1–2 days for a full court. Synthetic PU and acrylic systems take 5–7 days including curing time. Rubber flooring takes 2–3 days. Maple hardwood flooring requires 7–14 days for full installation including subfloor, laying, sanding, and multi-layer finishing. Court marking adds 1–2 additional days after the surface cures fully.
Do you offer warranty on basketball court installations?+
Yes — we offer industry-leading warranties on all installations. Maple hardwood: 15+ year structural warranty. Synthetic PU and acrylic: 10-year warranty. EPDM rubber: 10-year warranty. Modular PP tiles: 15-year material warranty. All warranties cover manufacturing defects, delamination, and surface failure under normal use. After-sales maintenance service is also available nationwide.
Which flooring is best for outdoor courts in Pakistan's climate?+
For outdoor courts in Pakistan, we recommend modular polypropylene tiles or acrylic cushion coating over concrete. Both withstand extreme heat (up to +80°C for PP tiles), UV radiation, monsoon rain, and heavy foot traffic without performance loss. PP tiles also add self-drainage and portability advantages. Hardwood is strictly for indoor use only.
Does court marking and line painting come included?+
Yes — all our basketball court installations include professional court marking as standard. We paint all basketball lines (3-point arc, free throw line, key/paint area, center circle, half-court line) to FIBA or NBA specifications. We also offer custom logos, team insignia, multi-sport markings, and branded court graphics at no extra design charge.
Can you install flooring over existing concrete?+
Absolutely. Most basketball flooring systems install directly over existing concrete, provided it is structurally sound, level (tolerance ≤3mm in 3m), and moisture-free. We conduct a full site assessment before installation and perform any required remedial work — leveling, moisture barriers, crack repairs. Modular tiles are the easiest option for existing concrete slabs.
