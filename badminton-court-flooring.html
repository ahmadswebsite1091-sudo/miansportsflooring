Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ANSWERED

10 years of customer questions, answered honestly. Still have a question? Call us — we answer within 1 hour.

What is the best badminton court flooring for Pakistan's climate? ▼
For Pakistan's hot, humid climate, PVC synthetic flooring is our top recommendation. It is moisture-resistant, thermally stable from -5°C to +55°C, requires minimal maintenance, and costs significantly less than hardwood. For air-conditioned premium facilities where budget allows, hardwood maple delivers the ultimate playing experience and prestige feel.
How long does badminton court flooring installation take? ▼
A single PVC badminton court takes 2–3 days from sub-floor preparation to court marking completion. Hardwood courts take 4–6 days. Multi-court projects are completed in parallel so total time increases only slightly. We always give you a firm completion date in the contract — and we keep it.
What is the total area required for a standard badminton court? ▼
A single BWF-standard badminton court measures 13.4m × 6.1m (81.74 sq.m). However, we recommend a total hall area of at least 16m × 9m (144 sq.m) per court to allow safe run-off space on all sides. For a 4-court hall, a minimum 20m × 30m indoor space is ideal.
How do I maintain my PVC badminton court flooring? ▼
PVC badminton courts are very low maintenance. Daily: dry sweep or dust mop to remove dust and debris. Weekly: damp mop with a mild neutral pH cleaner. Avoid harsh chemicals, solvents, or abrasive pads. Never use a high-pressure washer. Inspect adhesive edges monthly — any lifting edges should be re-glued promptly to prevent moisture entry.
What warranty do you provide on badminton court installations? ▼
All our badminton court installations come with a 5-year comprehensive written warranty covering material defects, surface delamination, adhesive failure, and court line fading. This is a formal written warranty document, not a verbal promise. We also offer annual maintenance contracts to extend the life of your court beyond 20 years.
Can you install multi-sport lines on a badminton court? ▼
Yes, absolutely. We can mark your court with badminton, basketball, volleyball, futsal, or any other sport's lines simultaneously using different BWF-approved colors to distinguish each sport's lines. This is very popular for school gymnasiums and multi-purpose sports halls where budget and space are shared.
Do you supply and install the badminton net posts as well? ▼
Yes. We supply and install BWF-approved net post sleeves embedded in the flooring during installation, along with tournament-grade adjustable net posts, nets, and post covers. Complete court packages including LED lighting recommendations and scoreboards are also available on request.
